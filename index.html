Hi! I'm Maya! I breathe all things tech! I'm a recent MBA grad from Harvard Business School ('23). Previously, I held a position as Sr. Product Manager at IFundWomen, a startup funding marketplace for women-led businesses, where I built thoughtful and intuitive products & experiences to empower female founders. As a skilled web and product developer, my specialty is taking an idea from concept to live, revenue generating MVPs. I've coached over 100 early stage entrepreneurs on how to build revenue generating MVPs. Prior to IFundWomen, I held positions at JPMorgan Chase, and Techstars Accelerator.

I'm also the Founder of Privy Labs , an open-source tech project building tools for a consent-focused internet. We are focused specifically on tooling to protect media content from harm & abuse including anti-piracy, deepfake detection, catfish prevention, & more. I'm also the Founder of COFFEE & CODED , a community empowering women to #trytech through skills-based workshops and classes in coding, design, and entrepreneurship.
